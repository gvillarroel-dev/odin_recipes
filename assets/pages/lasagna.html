How to cook a Lasagna?

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

This recipe will guide you through making a delicious homemade lasagna from scratch. It's packed with flavor and sure to impress your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, sauté until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet, cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, eggs, and a pinch of salt.
  7. Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella cheese, and meat sauce. Repeat layers.
  8. Top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  9. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
  11. Enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!

Now enjoy your Lasagna!
